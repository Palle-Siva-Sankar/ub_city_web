# Interview Demo Script

## 1) Context (20-30 sec)

"This is a browser-native commercial sales deck for UB City.  
The goal is to replace fragmented sales pitching (videos, static PDFs, and manual narration) with one interactive tool that works on live calls and standalone links."

## 2) Opening Impact (40-60 sec)

- Open `Overview`
- Speak to first-impression objectives:
  - immediate scale
  - energy
  - business intent
- Highlight top CTAs: leasing, sponsorship, venue booking

Suggested line:  
"Within the first screen, the user understands this is not a brochure site. It is a conversion-oriented commercial narrative."

## 3) Story Arc (2 min)

Move through:
- `Retail` (tenant and footprint narrative)
- `Luxury` (positioning and category differentiation)
- `Dining` (lifestyle demand and dwell-time logic)
- `Entertainment` + `Events` (traffic engine and platform value)

Suggested line:  
"Each section maps to a specific commercial argument, not just visual storytelling."

## 4) Conversion Paths (1.5 min)

Demonstrate:
- `/inquire/leasing`
- `/inquire/sponsorship`
- `/inquire/venue-booking`

Explain that CTA language and inquiry flow are aligned by business objective.

## 5) Expandability (1 min)

Open:
- `Sponsorship`
- `Leasing`
- `Venues`

Explain these as modular Phase 2-ready pathways with extensible routing/components.

## 6) Technical Summary (1 min)

- React + TypeScript + Router modular architecture
- Shared hero/media components + fallback behavior
- Global theme handling (dark/light accessibility fixes)
- Build/lint checks passed
- Performance hardening applied for animation/card smoothness

## 7) Close (20 sec)

"The final output is a productized sales tool: immersive, structured, and action-driven.  
It is designed to help a prospect decide and act, not just browse."
